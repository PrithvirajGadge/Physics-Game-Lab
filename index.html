Key Formulas

🚀 Projectile Motion
x = v·cos(θ)·t
Horizontal position at time t. No gravity effect horizontally — constant velocity.
y = h + v·sin(θ)·t − ½g·t²
Vertical position. h = launch height above ground. Gravity pulls down at g m/s².
R = v²·sin(2θ)/g
Horizontal range from ground level. Maximized at θ = 45°. Proportional to v².
H_max = v²·sin²(θ)/2g
Peak height above launch point, reached when vertical velocity = 0.
🚗 Relative Motion
Vr = V₁ − V₂
Relative speed (same direction). V₁ must exceed V₂ to overtake. Greater Vr = faster overtake.
D = L_A + L_B + gap
Total overtaking distance — both vehicle lengths plus the safety buffer.
t = D / Vr
Time to complete the overtake. Smaller relative speed = more time exposed to danger.
X = V × t
Distance each vehicle covers during overtake — used to verify safe clearance.
